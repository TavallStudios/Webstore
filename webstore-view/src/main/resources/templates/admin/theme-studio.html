Known runtime files

`theme.css` Loaded on every storefront page after the base storefront styles.
`theme.js` Loaded on every storefront page after the base storefront script.
`head.html` Injected into the `<head>` for extra tags, fonts, or metadata.
`body-top.html`, `body-bottom.html` Injected around the storefront body content for shared markup.
`home-top.html`, `home-bottom.html` Injected around the homepage story stack.
`product-top.html`, `product-bottom.html`, `footer.html` Injected around the product template and global footer.
